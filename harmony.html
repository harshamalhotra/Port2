HARMONY

The growth of internet has enabled a lot of artists, professional or not to showcase their creativity by selling their wares on various marketplace websites. A responsive e-commerce has improved their sales and Harmony is a peer-to-peer e-commerce website focused on handmade or vintage items by artists all around the world

DELIVARABLE

Responsive Web App

SKILLS AND RESPONSIBILITY

 UX Design, Brand Identity

TOOLS AND SOFTWARES

 Sketch, Illustrator,, Photoshop, Principle

THE CHALLENGE

DELIGHTFUL BROWSING
EASY CHECK OUT PROCESS

A responsive marketplace web application has a lot of information to display so that the customer can browse or find the product of their choice. To effectively manage this information and at the same time make the entire checkout process simple is a difficult solution to implement for Harmony.

IDENTITY AND BRANDING

DEFINING BRAND LANGUAGE

Harmony aims to exude a very friendly and elegant marketplace for buying unique items from artists. The logo is simple and aims to depict the energy and enthusiasm. The colors white and pinkish-red gives the app a very casual and trustworthy feel. Fonts Libre Baskerville and HK Grotesk, aim to give a classic and trustworthy feel.

INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

USER STORIES AND FLOW

To understand how the user is going to go through the entire checkout process, after signing up, a story board was drawn to understand how the process is going to be carried out. The flow included adding the  product in the cart and go ahead with the check out process.

WIREFRAMES

EXECUTION ON THE SCREENS

I sketched low fidelity wireframes to give a basic structure to the application.  Then, moved to Sketch to create  medium fidelity and high fidelity wireframes in Sketch for mobile, tablet and desktop experience.
